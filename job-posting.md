# Target Posting: Full Stack Developer — .NET and Shopify (Skullcandy)

Verbatim job posting this demo project is built against.

## Position Summary

Skullcandy is seeking a Full Stack Developer - .NET / Shopify to join our
Information Technology team. In this role, you will be the lead for coded
applications and will be responsible for assisting the IT team in
developing, maintaining, and optimizing new and legacy code across our
business applications, ecommerce platforms, websites, and APIs. This
includes planning, configuring, supporting, optimizing, and documenting
current and future applications.

The ideal candidate will have strong .NET development experience, a passion
for building applications and writing clean, maintainable code, and the
ability to support ecommerce development needs, including Shopify theme
customization, Liquid development, storefront updates, and integrations
with business systems. This role requires someone who can collaborate
across teams, troubleshoot technical issues, and carry projects from
conceptualization through completion.

This is a global role that serves all geographies and business units.

## Responsibilities

- Collaborate with departments and external partners to analyze software
  requirements and build new web applications, systems and processes.
- Provide front line support and ownership of .NET applications serving
  critical business functions and occasional after hours on call support
- Propose and administer improvements and additions to existing solutions.
- Define fundamental design principles for building a scalable application.
- Develop a strong understanding and knowledge of all different aspects of
  software projects.
- Create and prioritize projects via tracking systems
- Debug legacy code and refactor into maintainable solutions.
- Write clean, testable, readable, and easily maintainable code.
- Develop, customize, and maintain Shopify storefront functionality,
  including theme updates, product and collection page enhancements,
  promotional content, and ecommerce user experience improvements.
- Troubleshoot and update Shopify themes using Liquid, HTML, CSS, and
  JavaScript, ensuring changes are tested across desktop and mobile
  experiences.
- Support integrations between Shopify, third-party apps, APIs, and
  internal business systems.

## Experience & Qualifications

- BA or BS in Computer Science/Engineering, Mathematics or related field.
- 3+ years of professional development experience.
- Experience in web technologies: C#, ASP.NET, REST APIs, .NET Core, Entity
  Framework, IIS.
- Experience with JavaScript libraries such as jQuery.
- Experience developing, customizing, and maintaining Shopify storefronts,
  including Shopify theme architecture and best practices.
- Working knowledge of Shopify Liquid, including templates, sections,
  snippets, metafields, and dynamic content.
- Familiarity with Shopify admin tools, theme customization workflows,
  version control, and ecommerce functionality including product pages,
  collection pages, cart/checkout flows, and third-party app integrations.
- Familiarity with modern AI-assisted coding and development tools, such as
  Cursor, Claude Code, Google AI Studio, GitHub Copilot, or similar
  platforms, with the ability to use these tools responsibly to improve
  productivity, troubleshoot issues, document code, and support application
  development.
- Advanced knowledge of databases (we use Microsoft SQL Server): stored
  procedures, data modeling, querying and CRUD operations.
- Knowledge of scalable architecture with a strong understanding of
  microservices.
- Ability to take software engineering problems and present a solution with
  the capability to adjust based on feedback and business demands.
- A passion and drive to take ownership of problems, deliver quality
  solutions, stand out from the pack and grow with us.
- Strong understanding of business processes, experience working
  cross-departmentally with the ability to describe technical solutions to
  non-technical business leaders.
- Highly developed organizational and project management skills required to
  handle multiple, ongoing projects while keeping business leaders informed.
- Excellent communication skills, oral, written and interpersonal.
